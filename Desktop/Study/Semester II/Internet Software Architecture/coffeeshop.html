About Us

Established in 2010, The Singing Beans is a family-owned coffee shop that has been serving the local community with the finest quality coffee and tea.
We believe in using only the freshest and highest quality ingredients to make our beverages.
Our baristas are passionate about coffee and take pride in their craft. We strive to provide a warm and inviting
atmosphere for our customers to relax and enjoy their coffee.

Our core values are quality, consistency, and community.
We believe in providing our customers with a consistently excellent experience
every time they visit our shop. We also believe in giving back to the community by supporting local charities and events.
